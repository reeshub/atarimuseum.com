Atari: Christmas 1978
A Look at the Assembly Line

        As Christmas time grew nearer, the demand for Atari's comsumer games got louder and louder (as is traditional).   To help hush the toe-tapping buyers, several of Atari's supervisors, managers and other exempt employees from all divisions and walks of life pulled together.
