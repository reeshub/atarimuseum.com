Atari Television Commercials

(Airdates)


        From 1975 through 1984 Atari heavily advertised in magazines, newspapers, radio and especially television.   Atari became a household name only second company's such as IBM, Coke-Cola and moms apple pie.    Many of these commercials won awards, but more important spread the word of Atari around the world in a non-stop effort to ensure Atari's presence in everyone's home.   Below is a listing of commercials and when they were first aired and in what markets and shows.   This list incomplete and we will attempt to fill in the missing spots as we find more information.
