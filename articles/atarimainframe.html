Atari's IBM 370-148
MainFrame

 


        On December 6, 1978 another milestone was reached at Atari.   The IBM 370-148 computer system was installed at the company headquarters.   Over the next year, jobs being processed on the outside will be converted onto our new system.   This will enhance the turnaround time for all applications, and will provide more timely reports and meaningful decision-making information.   The IBM 370 computer is a system in the mid-range of IBM equipment capable of processing numerous applications simultaneously and still providing online teleprocessing for updating and retrievals of numerous data files and new program development.
