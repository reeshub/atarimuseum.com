The Atari CX-22 PROLINE TrakBall

]
            The Atari CX-22 Proline Trakball controller was designed for the XL series of home computers and was dressed in XL Brown/Creme colors.   It had a switch on the left side to set it from Trakbal to Joystick mode so you could choose which way you wanted to use the trakball.   Just like the 5200 Trakball, this was a very well designed, comfortable and reliable product.
