The Atari CX-77 Touch Tablet

        The Atari Touch Tablet was a well designed and brilliant idea.   Giving the user the ability to use a virtual sheet of paper and pen, they could draw, drag and drop, paint and erase images with the included Atari Artist program.   Perhaps given more time, the Touch Tablet could have been used for many other innovative ideas and perhaps vertical market applications.   The Atari CX-77 Touch Tablet was designed by Tom Palecki formerly of Atari's Industrial Design group.   Tom still has his own personal CX-77 on display in his office at Pear Design, Inc. in California.
