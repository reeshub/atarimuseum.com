Atari 1090 Prototyping/Hobbyist Card

(From Atari Engineering. Populated, unknown function.)

Prototyping Card - Front

 

 

Atari 1090 Prototyping/Hobbist Card.  One of the many announced and

planned cards for the 1090 XL Expansion System.   The board has all

the needed traces and footprints for Ground, Power and a voltage

regulator.  Plenty of space to place lots of components.

 

This particular Prototyping card was in fact used for Prototyping an

unknown Expansion Card.  It has Static RAM and a Character Set

ROM on it besides all of the TTL logic.  Its been testing in a 1090

and it does not appear to present any features.   Hopefully an Atari

engineer familiar with this particular board will contact the Atari

Museum and the mystery can be solved.
