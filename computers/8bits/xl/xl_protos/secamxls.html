The Atari SECAM 600/800XL Systems
        Atari had worked on making sure its line of computer systems could work worldwide, so they designed a seperate set of XL computers specifically for use with French SECAM video standards, here are the 2 original prototype motherboards which are bare, the SECAM version of the 600XL was nicknamed Pauline.   The NTSC version of the 600XL was nicknamed "SHIRLEY" at the WCI New York Atari Labs and "SURELY" in the Sunnyvale, CA. home computer division.
