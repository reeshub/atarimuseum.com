Unkown Prototype Bottom Case

        This rather unusual looking bottom case plastic is the same size as the Atari 1200/1400XL and the cartridge port is shaped in a different manor that a 1200XL side cartridge port plastic will not fit and the above shown 1400XL cartridge port does fit, but not perfectly.    The center of the rear of the bottom case has what appears to be a Power or Monitor port and next to it an SIO port.   On the sides are large openings, perhaps for some form of plug in modules perhaps, its unknown.   So far, noone in Atari's Industrial Design Group can identify what this bottom computer case was meant for.
