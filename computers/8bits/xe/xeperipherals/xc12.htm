The Atari XC12

 

 

The Atari XC12
Program Recorder Tape Drive

   Atari, in Europe sold a great number of these Program Recorders. A replacement for the original Atari 410 and the later released Atari 1010 Tape drives. The XC12 matched the styling of the Atari XE series of computers released in 1985-1988. The European computer market consumer did not have the spending dollars of their US counterparts, so the disk drives were outside the budget of many eager Atari computer enthusiasts, so an economical alternative was the program recorder.

    Due to such heavy use of this peripherals, many hardware hackers modified the units to increase their transfer speed to give data saves and loads a boost in speed.
