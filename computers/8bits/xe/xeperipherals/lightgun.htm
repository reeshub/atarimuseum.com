The Atari XE Lightgun

 

 

The Atari XE System Lightgun. (XG-1) Was sold as a seperate package.   Originally this accessory and the "Bug Hunt" cartridge were packaged together with the Atari XE Game System.    To allow existing Atari 400/800, XL/XE computer owners the chance to also use this accessory and games designed for use with it, Atari sold it seperately.    The lightgun also worked with the Atari 7800 ProSystem as well with games that were compatible. A version of the XG-1 was also made in Orange colored plastic.
