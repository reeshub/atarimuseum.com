Atari Portable System

 
 




        All in one portable computer in 1983 with flourescent of LCD display, a fold-up keyboard, built in 3.5" disk drive and an 80 column printer.   Quite an impressive concept for the traveling user who would have it all in one convenient carry-around package.    Sort of looks like a cross between the Compaq Portable and some of today's all-in-one wordprocessors.
