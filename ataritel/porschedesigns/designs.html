ATARITEL Phones

        Thanks to Karl Morris, the Atari Historical Society European contact, these never before seen photo's of sketches and photo's of Ataritel phones designed by Porsche Designs are now available below to view.    Karl was able to obtain these images by working with Porsche Designs, one of the two companies contracted by Atari to work along with Atari's own Industrial Design group to create a line of high tech and stylish phones with features and styling never before seen in home phones.
