Atari Commercials
Story Boards
        During the "Warner Days" of Atari a big push on Television commercials was done by marketing for Atari's line of video games and home computers.   Included with many dealer sales books were Story Boards of commercials that Atari was planning to air so that dealers could see what products Atari was advertising and what story line was being used to sell a product to the public.   Below are several Story Boards from a few of the Commercials Atari aired in the 1980's.
