:: Offsite Services ::
 

While your firm may do its best in backing up its data on a daily, weekly and monthly basis. Your firm even goes so far as to make duplicate sets of your backup tapes for Compliance. What good is your backup data to you if it is kept in the same physical location as your original source equipment and data in the event of an disaster?!?!

In today's environment you can not take any type of chances with your data. Fire, Floods, Blackouts, acts of God or even worse are always looming in the dark corners waiting to spring upon your business to disable or completely destroy your most valuable assets.... your data...  

Account receivables, client data, prototype design materials and much more.  Does your firm need to place materials in Escrow, hold backup data in storage during a migration to a new location, or to rebuild from a major disaster?    We can provide you safe, secure and reliable offsite storage and rotational access.

 

 

The Legacy Engineering Group solves this critical issue with its offsite "SAFEVAULT-DATA" Tape/Data Storage solutions.

Using Humidity/Temperature controlled storage, we can provide your backup data a safe offsite location for peace of mind, so your firm can maintain stringent Compliance requirements or to know you have emergency backup data in the event of a disaster.  From 1 to 1,000 Backup Tapes, Hard Drives, or other forms of Media our newly opened 2,000 Sq Ft and expanding initial Storage Center is ready to meet your demands.

 

 

Heavy Rotational Tape Schedule?   Not a problem...    For clients with Rotational Tape needs we offer Turtle crates and transport cases.    Made from High Impact plastic, these water-tight cases are perfect for month to month storage or shipping to-from our SafeVault-Data facility to you or your clients.   You receive a monthly report of your inventory, tapes rotated in/out and an average temperature/humidity report.
