Atari Super Pong
Pro-Am Ten
Model # C-202
 

        Atari's last version of the Super Pong line.   Super Pong Pro-Am (Professional - Amateur) Ten.   Up to 4 players and up to 10 different games.   With Pro or Am Difficulty Selector for beginners or more advanced game play, On-screen digital scoring, vivid color on color TV's and the original PONG sound you could play either Pro or Amateur version of Pong, Super Pong, Catch, Basketball and Handball.   Atari's PONG home dedicated consoles just kept getting better and better, even if the "PONG" theme was getting very long in the tooth.    Year: 1977.
