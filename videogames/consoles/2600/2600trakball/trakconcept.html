Atari Trakball Concepts

        All of us generally are familiar with the standard Atari CX-80 and CX-22 Trakball controllers which can be used on any Atari 2600, 7800 and Atari home computer system with a compatible DB-9 controller port.    The Atari Trakball is a very well designed and is ergonomically shapped to sit as well on a tabletop or in a persons laptop for easy use.   However most people never get the opportunity to look at the concept drawings and artwork of an Atari product as it is being conceived and planned.   Below are 15 various concept designs of the Atari trakball up to it's final design.
