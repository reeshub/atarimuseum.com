Home (photo) Page — Wanda at Atari Reunion 1997

Howdy, y'all! It was good seeing everybody on Wednesday, August 13, at San Jose Live! for Atari's 25th Anniversary reunion. It's hard to believe that it was over twenty years ago that I signed on. Even though I've long since left (Early 1980), I remember my times there fondly. Some of you I've known since my first day. A few of us have changed a bit, a few of us have nearly time-frozen. I can hardly believe some of my peers—grandparents! As for me, I'm in arrested development!

Anyway, I'd promised a few Atarians that I'd let them know when I got a reunion page up. Well, here it is! Since I don't want to cause all sorts of excessive download times, this page has the thumbnails (sort of big, but they need to be). Click on them to link to the pages to see the bigger images, along with identification and next/previous/home links. I couldn't identify everyone... sorry.

Please forward this URL to other Atarians! Thanks!
