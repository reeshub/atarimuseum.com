10/27/1998 Update




        Things are starting to pick up again here at the Atari Historical Society website.     Many new additions and updates are in the process of being added.   Many older links which were not active are being brought on-line and many new additions are being made.    Currently a meeting with a dozen or so ex-Atari engineers and employees has been scheduled for October 31 - November 3.    During these meetings it is hoped that many questions will be answered, new hardcopy proof of projects will be revealed, new hardware will be located and shown and a lot of great stories will be told.    Once these meetings have been concluded this additional information and hardware will be examined, recorded, photographed and presented to the public here on the site.    We have invited the staff of Atari Gamming Headquarters to assist in examining and cataloging this new data and equipment once it has been brought back to our New York museum.
