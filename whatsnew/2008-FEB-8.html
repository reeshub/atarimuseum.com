AtariMuseum - Site News:   February 8, 2008

 

Greeting fellow Atari enthusiasts...

http://www.atarimuseum.com/computers/8BITS/XL/1600xl/1600xl.html

   In the Atari ST Section in the Atari EST prototype page, some new information has been added regarding its MMU chip:
