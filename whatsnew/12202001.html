Atari History Site Online Museum: December 20, 2001 Update
 
 

        Hope everyone enjoyed the 5 Part series Atari 1200XL Promotional video.   Our apologies on the whine in the background, the conversion from a betamax tape to a VHS format kept bringing out that background noise and resulted in it being in the AVI's, not much could be done.

         Now for you Jaguar 64 fans.....   those who love to explore the mystery and lore of the legendary precursor to the Jaguar, the Atari Panther will enjoy this latest update.    Included in the Atari Jaguar 64 section under the Atari Jaguar Technical Data & Documents section is a WinZIP file containing 95k of directories and disk images with developers software, notes, utilities and the infamous "Panther Pong" which is actually a crude Breakout game in reality.    Enjoy.... more Panther information to come.
 

These pieces of Atari Panther data and docs are curteousy of our friends from the former East Coast Atari dealer... Toad Computers very own David & Jennifer Troy, thanks and Happy Holidays Dave & Jen!!!!!
