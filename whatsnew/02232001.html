February 23, 2001 Update: The Atari History Site
 

A new page has been add the The Other Atari Projects section called: Atari-McDonalds "Taste the Thrill of Atari" Promotion which includes images of the original posters that were displayed in the McDonald's restuarants, actual scratch game pieces and the 1982 television commercial.

In the Atari Television Commericals archive is a new commercial, its not by Atari though, its by M-Network (the 3rd software division of Mattel Electronics) and its called "M Networks got the cure for the video blues"   Its a commercial showing the lineup of new games M Network produced for the Atari 2600 VCS, its an 80's pop does 50's rock commercial.
