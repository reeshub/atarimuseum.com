May 16, 2000 Update:
 
 

    Hi everyone.    The site has had several minor updates.
 

In the Atari ST/TT/Falcon section the Atari Falcon030 page has been finally fixed and added back into the website.
In the Atari 400/800 peripherals section, the Atari CX-85 Numeric Keypad has been updated with a new image.
In the Atari Standalone Consoles section, the Atari C-380 Video Pinball image has been updated.
In the Atari Jaguar 64 section, the Atari JagVR system has been added with a link to a demonstration video from the 1995 E3.
In the Atari Links section, the entire page has been reformatted and several new links have been added.
Karl Morris has created several new page headers for the Whats New, About AHS and Atari Links sections of the website which look really nice.
Most noticable is the update to the opening webpage graphic which now includes characters from many of Atari's coin-op through home console games.
 

   For those looking for news on the Atari 7800 High Score Cartridge, as of yesterday I have placed the whole project on hold.   The batteries are draining out in only 3 weeks and until I can find a solution to correct the problem without having to go back and redesign the boards to accomodate the fix the sale of them is on hold.    If a board change needs to be made the cartridges will go up in price from $59.95 to $64.95 since is will cost another almost $500 in setup costs to make another run of boards plus I'll have to order the new boards, desolder all of the test run carts and rebuild a new batch.   I am looking into non-volitale SRAM from simtek, if it works out and they can give me a good price on quantity, that may be the route to go.    For those curious as to what they look like, screen shots, the cartridge label and so forth, please visit the Atari 7800 HSC Update Page.
