June 29, 2000 Update:
 

        For those not aware and interested in purchasing, the new 7800 High Score cartridges are now available, they are $59.95 each and are available exclusively through B&C Atari Sales and Video 61 Atari Sales.    The cartridge plugs into your Atari 7800 PROsystem console and then you plug any of the 9 original 1984 titles ontop of the High score cartridge, the games that are compatible are:

Asteroids
Dig Dug
Joust
Xevious
Ms. Pac Man
Galaga
Centipede
Food Fight
Robotron: 2084
 

    To learn more about the 7800 High Score cartridge for the Atari 7800 PROsystem, visit the 7800 High Score Cart webpage.
 

    Just added to the Atari Articles section of the website under Atari Commercials is a new page about the 2 minute commercial created by Abel & Associates for Atari which was shown in movie theatres during the previews before the movie Star Wars: Return of the Jedi was shown.    Its called Atari "The Fly".  The 2 minute commercial shows Yars' Revenge, Asteroids and Star Raiders and is a work of art in the mixture of real life and computer animation in 1983.   At the end of the article is a link to view the actual commercial which is 1 min, 57 secs and is a 3.3mb AVI file.
