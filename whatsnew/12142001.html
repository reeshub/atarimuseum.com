Atari History Site: Online Museum December 14, 2001 Update
 
 

Our great friends Albert and Alex over at www.atariage.com and the AHS have worked out a new cross-linking arrangement.    The AHS now has in its various sub-menu's links directly into Atariage's message boards which pertain to each topic such as Atari 2600, 8bit computers, coin-ops and so forth.    So now when you visit the AHS and go to look up some news or info, you can also then join the discussions happening in the atariage.com message boards then select BACK to the AHS to keep reading and researching....
