Atari History Site Online Museum: February 27, 2002 Update
 
 

    I thought it was time for an update announcement, even though the updates aren't exactly finished yet.    Karl and I have been completely reworking the entire Atari Commercials Archives into a new section of the website called the Video Vault.    This new section overhaul was needed due the larger number of new commercials which have been added and are still being converted and added into the new section...   Many links are still not completed while others only link to a single video file.   We are working on both high and low bandwidth versions of the videos and thanks to some assistance from the Digital Press editor, we are also looking into streaming out video which would be in a higher quality format.

   The new commercials are some real milestones and eye openers:
 

Atari's first ever TV Commercial: Atari Home Pong which aired in 1976
Atari's Super Pong console, also in 1976
8 Atari 5200 Commercials which are truley amazing!!!
A 1977 Atari VCS commercial
Several Atari 2600 1982 & 1983 commercials
The first teaser commercial for the Atari Jaguar 64 aired in 1993

...also the 2nd part of the Jack Tramiel Interview...................
 

    The Vault Video is still under construction and still being updated over the next week of so, so please bare with any missing links and such as the section is brought up to full speed.... enjoy all of the great new media in the section.
