February 19, 2001 Update: The Atari History Site
 
 

Jaguar 2 Specifications provided curteousy of Markus Kirschbaum

Matthew Reichert now has the honor of maintaining the Atari 2600 Label Variations FAQ which was created and formerly maintained by the legendary classic collector John Earney.   Matt has provided the AHS with the current FAQ and hopefully we will see regular updates of this extensive and exhaustive resource on the numerous label variations of 2600 video game cartridge labels.

Richard Miller, one of the architects of the Atari Jaguar 64, had keep a composition book of notes on the development of the fabled Atari Jaguar 64 MPEG-1 Video CD Movie cartridge.    This book has been scanned and turned into an Adobe PDF file for all to read and examine.
