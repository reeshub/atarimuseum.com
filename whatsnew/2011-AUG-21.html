AtariMuseum - Site News:   August 21, 2011

 

Greeting fellow Atari enthusiasts...

       Hey, another year and one whole update!  Woo Hoo.... okay, seriously, I'm really sorry, the website may not be updated, but here in the offices a lot has been going on, the massive archival effort took nearly a year.    The office now has 2 fireproof storage rooms for the archives, things have been sorted out into architecture tubes, folders have been sorted and consolidated by product and things are a heck of a lot easier and more manageable.

 

      Well, over on Atariage in their 8bit computers section, a lot of talk has been going on about the 1400XL and 1450XLD computers.    So I am going to start pulling out a great deal of technical materials that are not as of yet ever been seen by the public and put them up.   Tonight just quickly I scanned in the PAL (Programmable Array Logic) chips that are in the 1400XL computers and posted up their Decoding and their Equations, this can be very helpful to those looking to understand further these outstanding machines.
