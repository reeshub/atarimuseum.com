March 29, 2000 Update
 
 

    Hi everyone, well this has been a long promised addition to the site which I have received numerous email requests constantly asking when it would be made available.    Please have a look at the new section in the Atari 1200XL section of the website.   Concept sketches of the original Atari 1200 Designs and its sister designs, the Atari A-300 project.    I think everyone who is a computer collector and interested in Atari's computer history will enjoy this truly eye opening exhibit of the evolution and creation of the XL look and the 1200XL design, but also a look at an alternative modular expandable computer system Atari had considered for a short bit but in the end chose not to go forward with.
