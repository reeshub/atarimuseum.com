March 5, 2001 Update: The Atari History Site
 

        New update in the Atari Jaguar 64 Technical Data page:  NETLIST Files.    These files are from John Mathieson, one of the architects of the Atari Jaguar 64.   These files came from John through Glenn Bruner.    The NETLIST files are the files that were used to create the TOM & JERRY chipset.

        New in the Atari 7800 PROsystem page:  Two new technical documents from Bill Hofmann and Kevin Osborn who are former General Computer Company employees.    The two new documents are Maria-2 Hand Hints and 3600 Software Guide.    These two documents could come in very handy for developing software for the Atari 7800.

        You might be saying "Well, thats all and good, but without the ability to create a checksum key and put it into my software, I wont be able to run it on a stock Atari 7800"   Well, very shortly there will be an announcement regarding the checksum key calculation, stay tuned for the great news !!!
