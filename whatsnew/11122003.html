AHS - Site News:   November 12, 2003

 

Jaguar 64 Console encryption files found for 10th anniversary:

 

    The Atari Museum is very happy to announce that at no better time then the Atari Jaguar 64's 10th anniversary, that the elusive Jagcrypt files have at long last been located and are now available to the Jaguar Developers community.

   Finally carts and CD's will now be released that no longer will require bypass carts or modified Jaguar consoles.

  To download the ZIP files containing the encryption programs for both cart and CD-ROM, go to:
