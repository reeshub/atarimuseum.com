Atari History Site Online Museum: May 3, 2002 Update
 

        Whoa!!!   So What is new?!?!?    Gotten a lot of email over the months, "When's the next Update?!?!?"    Whats been going on at the Atari History Museum???

        That can be answered simply..... A LOT !!!    While there has not been on-line additions at the AHS, a lot has been going on in the real world having to do with Atari and its history.     While it would be great to have a real and permanent museum which has been a dream here at the AHS for a long time, the cost and logistics are just to high at this time, so instead Curt Vendel has donated several coin-op games to the Fun Spot NH (www.funspotnh.com) Classic Coin-op Museum.

        To see more about the donation of the coin-ops, CLICK HERE

        So what else is new?     The Game On Exposition in London will be taking place later this year, so the AHS has placed on loan to the exhibit a Nutting Yellow Computer Space.     Recently the trucking company came to pick up the Computer Space for shipment to London.      A week later Curt Vendel was contacted by Game On, the COmputer Space arrived safe and sound and is being prepared for the exposition, for photo's CLICK HERE
 

        Its been quite a few months, anything else been going on???      Sure thing!      Check out the Atari Museum exhibit at this past weekends Phillyclassic 3 Show.   CLICK HERE             Also, for those who are handy at woodworking will want to check out the step by step tutorial on how the 3 Store display Kiosks were built for the Phillyclassic show:

Reproducing Classic Gaming Store Display Kiosks

        So, now as for the site?   Not to fear, lots and I mean LOTS of new updates coming quite soon including more detailed photo's of the original all black prototype of the Atari Video System X, plus incredible photo's of Atari's never released Handheld Space Invaders and Breakout games.    More doc's, info, flyers and photo's to follow!
