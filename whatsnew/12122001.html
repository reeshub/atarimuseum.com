Atari History Site: Online Museum Update: December 12, 2001
 
 

    Ok, so here goes update #1 of 12 for the month of December as promised in the previous update.     Already on-line were photo's of the actual prototype of the Atari Bionics System, the modular concept medical system designed by Regan Cheng formerly with Atari's Industrial Design team.    The Bionics system was the birthplace design for the muscular electrical reading module which would later be turned into the Atari Mindlink controller...   Now available at the bottom of the page is a link to a group of 16 prototype concept sketches of the Bionics system.
