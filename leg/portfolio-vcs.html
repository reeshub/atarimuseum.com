Design and build: Flashback console :: Client - Atari, Inc.

  
   
 

We have worked with Atari/Infogrames for over 10 years.  Infogrames was one of the worlds largest publishers and developers of videogames.

We designed and developed the new Atari Flashback and Flashback 2 TV videogame consoles.  As part of the development we utilized legacy properties that were only in existence in - "The Atari Museum" archives and updated the original 1977 technology into a single modern package realizing a new revenue stream for Atari.  

The product line was a huge success, selling over 860,000 domestic US units in 2005 alone.  We were commissioned to do a 2+ follow up and we also developed a handheld LCD portable which was not picked up by Atari.
