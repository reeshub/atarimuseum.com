Remake of the Atari 7800 console :: Client - O'Shea LTD / Atari, Inc.

  
   
 

We were commissioned in late 2002 through 2003 to design a fully working  reproduction of the 1984-1992 Atari 7800 ProSystem that would be completely identical in its functionality and compatibility with all  existing Atari 2600 and 7800 game cartridges.

The PCB layout team created a new motherboard layout.

Our Industrial Design Team created a newly updated look to the original  product line to help the product stand on its own and to distinguish it  from the original Atari 7800 model. This included a beefy top section,  oval buttons, and a curve-over clear power indicator.    The project  was completed and delivered to the client.    The client never produced the product for retail sale.   10 Grey units and 3 special edition pearl white units were built.
