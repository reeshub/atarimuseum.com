Consultation: Fossil Watches :: Client - Fossil, Inc.

  
   
 

We worked with watch maker "Fossil" to ensure the accurate representation of various famous videogames for its collectors line.

It took many hours of work with the LCD glass manufacturer to ensure the moving displays were 100% accurate.

Asteroids, Breakout and Centipede were among some of the LCD representations produced.
