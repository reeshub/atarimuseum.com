The Movie

 

 

   Starring Henry Thomas, Dabney Coleman and Michael Murphy.    This movie was an Atari lovers dream come true.  

A movie centering around a young boy who by chance stumbles into a ring of espionage and murder.   A real life Cloak & Dagger for a boy who, because of the loss of his mother and a distant relationship with his father, is living day to day in his dreams and fantasies only to now find them becoming real.     All the while, the very object of desire for the spies is secret government data contained within an Atari 5200 Supersystem video game cartridge.    To access this hidden material, one must play the Cloak & Dagger video game and achieve that magic score -
